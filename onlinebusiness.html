My Journey

My Journey to Momentum

Growing up, I felt like I was constantly searching for something more. I had big dreams but no clear path to reach them. I spent years trying different approaches, making mistakes, and learning from every setback.

Through my own journey of personal growth, I discovered that success isn't about having all the answers — it's about having the courage to keep moving forward, even when things get tough. It's about building momentum, one small step at a time.

My latest 'small step' was moving away from the traditional roster and toward a future-proof digital model. Let's be real: the old way of working isn't just exhausting; it's becoming obsolete. I chose to align myself with a growth industry and join a business that prioritises impact over hours.

Now, I'm on a mission to help other young people like me do the same. Because I believe that everyone deserves to feel empowered, confident, and excited about their future.
